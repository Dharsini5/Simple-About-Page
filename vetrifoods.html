ABOUT US

Established in 1972, Vetrii Foods, a venture of Saraswathi Consumer Product, champions 48 years of culinary excellence. Committed to delivering authentic taste, our ISO-certified brand crafts a diverse range of Agro Food and FMCG products under the name VETRII, ensuring quality and satisfaction in every bite.
